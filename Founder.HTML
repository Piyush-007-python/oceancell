About the Founder

Piyush Jakhar is the visionary behind the OceanCell™ Sustainable Project — a transformative solution designed to extract green hydrogen, oxygen, and essential by-products from seawater using renewable energy. Driven by a mission to revolutionize energy, water, and sanitation systems globally, Piyush’s innovation empowers local communities, supports climate action, and promotes global sustainability through science and cooperation.
